About Anna Sheer

Anna is a certified Bodysex®️ coach through the Betty Dodson Association in New York and offers emancipating sex education through pleasure-focused coaching, workshops, and retreats for women and female bodied people. Her mission is to support women in removing shame and guilt around their sexual pleasure and replacing it with self-knowledge—particularly around the female body and the female model of arousal. She was fortunate to grow up in a sex-positive family, where sex was never a taboo subject. Instead, it was seen as a natural and normal part of life, something that could be openly discussed. It was only later, through her work as a sex educator, that she realised how rare and special that upbringing was.

She has worked with numerous women who have benefited from a life-changing perspective about their relationship with themselves, their bodies, sexuality and pleasure. Anna is an artist and an educator who believes self-knowledge is the key to empowerment, confidence, and independence.

Anna has spent over two decades working as a facilitator and teacher across the cultural sector in operas, theatres, museums, schools, and academies. Since 2012, she is the CEO of sowieso!—an inclusive association offering art and dance workshops for children with disabilities, always placing creativity at the heart of her practice.

“Changing the world one orgasm at a time”—a quote by Betty—has enriched Anna’s charismatic mission to support people in gaining the knowledge to lead confident, sexual relationships with themselves and others.

About Dr. Corynna Clarke

Dr. Corynna Clarke is a holistic health practitioner with extensive experience in various healing modalities. She specializes in integrating traditional therapies with modern practices to support her clients' holistic wellness. Corynna is passionate about educating individuals on their health and well-being, empowering them to take charge of their lives.

As a certified tantric educator, she offers courses and workshops that delve into the healing power of sexuality and awareness, providing transformative experiences for her clients. With a deep understanding of body mapping and energy work, she brings a unique perspective to her practice, helping individuals connect with their true selves.

Dr. Clarke believes that healing is a journey and is committed to guiding others on their path to self-discovery and wellness.

About Astarte

She is dedicated to helping her clients navigate their personal journeys of healing and empowerment, fostering a deep sense of self-awareness and connection.

Astarte’s mission is to inspire individuals to reclaim their bodies and stories, offering tools and support for lasting transformation and growth.
